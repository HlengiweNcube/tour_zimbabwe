About Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a landlocked country in Southern Africa, blessed with spectacular natural wonders, rich wildlife, and deep cultural heritage. From the roaring Victoria Falls to the ancient Great Zimbabwe ruins, every corner tells a story of resilience and beauty.

Whether you’re drawn to the bustling streets of Harare, the tranquil waters of Lake Kariba, or the diverse wildlife of Hwange National Park, Zimbabwe promises an unforgettable experience.

Top Destinations

Victoria Falls

Experience the thunderous beauty of one of the world's largest waterfalls.

Hwange National Park

Home to vast herds of elephants and incredible wildlife.

Lake Kariba

One of the world's largest man-made lakes — perfect for fishing, sunsets, and boat safaris.

Matobo Hills

Marvel at balancing rocks and ancient cave paintings.

Great Zimbabwe Ruins

Explore the ancient stone city, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Bulawayo

The City of Kings – Zimbabwe’s cultural and historical capital.

Harare

Zimbabwe’s vibrant capital — full of art, culture, and hospitality.
